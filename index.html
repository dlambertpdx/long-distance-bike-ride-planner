Long Distance Bike Ride Planner

  • This app will allow you to plan a bike route for rides intended to last an hour or more.
  • The user can can enter origin and destination points, and get a route, estimated miles, and turn-by-turn directions.
  • In addition, the user's route will display a marker that indicates hour increments.
  • The user's route will also display bike shops in proximity to the hourly stop markers.
